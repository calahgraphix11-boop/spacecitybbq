Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Space City B·B·Q LLC ("Space City BBQ," "we," or "us") handles information when you use this website. We keep it simple, because we collect very little.

What we collect

  • When you place a pickup order through this site, you enter your name and a preferred pickup time. The site uses your own device's email app to send that order to us — it does not store your information on the website itself.
  • When you email us, we receive your email address and anything you include in your message.
  • We do not use accounts, passwords, or logins on this site.

Payments

Payment is handled in person at pickup, or through our Square online store. We do not collect or store card numbers on this site. When you pay through Square, your information is handled under Square's own privacy policy.

How we use your information

Only to prepare and confirm your pickup order and to respond to your messages. Nothing else.

Sharing

We do not sell your information. We share it only as needed to fill your order — for example, with our email provider, or with Square when you pay online.

Cookies and analytics

As built, this site does not use tracking cookies or advertising trackers.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Your choices

To ask what information we have about you, or to request that we delete it, email us at the address below.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above shows the latest version.
